About The Painting

Painted in 1889, The Starry Night became one of the most iconic artworks in history. Van Gogh painted it while staying at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in France.


The swirling night sky creates a dreamlike atmosphere, while the glowing stars and moon symbolize hope, emotion, imagination, and beauty beyond reality.


Its magical combination of deep blues and glowing yellows makes the artwork feel alive — almost as if the sky itself is moving.

Artist

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter known for emotional and expressive artworks.

Year

The painting was created in June 1889 during one of the most emotional moments of his life.

Meaning

Many believe the painting represents dreams, hope, loneliness, and the beauty of imagination.

Colors

Soft glowing yellows blend beautifully with deep calming blues to create visual harmony.
